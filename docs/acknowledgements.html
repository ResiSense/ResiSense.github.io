                            Sponsors

                            The following organizations have provided financial support to the project.

                            Opentrons


                            Opentrons has kindly provided us with credits worth 25000USD, with which we acquired a liquid handling robot and various accompanying equipment for our experiments.

                            Integrated DNA Technologies


                            Integrated DNA Technologies has kindly provided us with credits worth 3400USD, with which we acquired primers and gene fragments for our experiments.

                            New England Biolabs


                            New England Biolabs has kindly provided us with reagents totalling 880EUR for our experiments.

                            Hong Kong Life Sciences Society


                            The Hong Kong Life Sciences Society has kindly provided us with 5000HKD to support our project.

                            Hong Kong Amgen Biotech Experience


                            Hong Kong Amgen Biotech Experience has kindly provided us with various cells, plasmids, and reagents totalling 1000HKD for our experiments.
